(some of the art on these pages aren't technically ascii, just dot art) ascii is loosely used to refer to internet text based art, but it also has a strict definition. ascii art is defined by the "ascii standard" which consists of 128 characters used to create images.

text art existed long before ascii, people made images using typewriters and wrote paragraphs to be shaped in specific ways. this dates all the way back to ancient greece!

ascii art first emerged in the late 1970s when computers were becoming more available. computers were very limited and terminals didn't have image options so people used characters instead.

ascii became more and more popular during the early internet and peaked sometime in the 90s. but it is still used today.
